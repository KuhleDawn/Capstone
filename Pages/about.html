About

Component Detector is an innovative mobile application designed for mechanics and quality assurance professionals to easily detect mechanical faults in components such as Shafts, Rod Ends, Clutches, Washers, Bolts, and Nuts. It integration of visual inspection and fault detection through image recognition technology significantly enhances the precision and effectiveness of mechanical component evaluations. By leveraging these features, mechanics and quality assurance professionals can conduct thorough inspections, detect faults accurately, and make informed maintenance decisions, ultimately improving the reliability and longevity of mechanical systems. It allows users to easily upload pictures from their device's gallery or directly capture images within the app. I also utilize machine learning algorithms to analyze the images and identify potential faults such as misalignment, wear and tear, corrosion, cracks, and more. It is designed to be user-friendly and enable mechanics and quality assurance professionals to efficiently utilize the app in their daily operations. this ensuring time is managed and preventing potential failures
