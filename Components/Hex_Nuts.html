Hex_Nuts

Hex nuts, also known simply as hexagonal nuts or hexagon nuts, are a common type of fastener used to secure two or more objects together by creating a threaded connection with a bolt or threaded rod.

More about Hex_Nuts

These nuts have a distinctive hexagonal shape, which provides several advantages, including ease of tightening and loosening with standard wrenches or pliers. Hex nuts have internal threads that match the threads on the corresponding bolt or threaded rod. These threads come in various standardized sizes and pitches to accommodate different applications and types of bolts. Hex nuts are typically six-sided, which makes them easy to grip and turn with common hand tools like wrenches and socket sets. This shape allows for better torque application, ensuring a secure and tight connection.
